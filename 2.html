The road to the mother


Description


The plot of the film "Road to the mother" lay tale about the power of maternal love, which helped the protagonist to survive and cope with difficult tests on the way home. The plot involves several complex historical periods of Kazakhstan: the era of collectivization, the greatest tragedy of the nation — jute, the years of the great Patriotic war and postwar years. The main character in the picture Ilyas demonstrates the incredible vitality of the spirit, courage, patriotism and unparalleled dedication. He will have to face the hard blows of fate: the orphanage, harsh war, captivity, prison camp, but nothing can stop him right in front of mother. The movie makes you think about life values and to rethink the view of the world.
